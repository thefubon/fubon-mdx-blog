# Night Stroll

3D environment showcasing a wet, rainy night in New York City. This fully computer-generated scene captures the atmospheric mood of Madison Avenue with realistic rain effects, reflections, and ambient lighting.

## Project Details

- Rendered in Unreal Engine 5
- Photorealistic lighting setup with custom volumetrics
- Procedural rain and water surface systems
- Post-processing for cinematic look and feel
